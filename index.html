Why Choose us

Singapore is home to the best speech language therapy services in the region. Speech Therapy Works focuses brining you hope and help while ensuring evidence-based approach.

Bringing Hope and Help

Community Based

Speech Therapy Works is a community-based private practice serving clients across all ages especially families or individuals who have difficulty receiving therapy, or would benefit from having the therapist come to them.

Home Therapy

We aim to be the leading home based speech therapy provider offering high-quality personalized speech therapy to our clients in the comfort of their own homes.

Experienced Professionals

Our vast experience in both the private and public healthcare sector allows us to have a better understanding of Singapore’s overall healthcare system.

Multi Dialect

We are able to reach out to the community across different language and dialect groups as we are proficient in English, Malay, Mandarin, Cantonese, Hokkien and Hainanese.
